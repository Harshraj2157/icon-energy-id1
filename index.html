About Us

Icon Energy, established in 2016, is a leading provider of solar energy solutions, committed to revolutionizing India’s renewable energy landscape. Headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, with branch offices in Mehsana, we specialize in delivering end-to-end Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) services for residential, commercial, industrial, and large-scale solar projects.

Over the years, we have successfully installed 10,000+ Solar Projects, earning our place as one of Gujarat’s top EPC companies in 2024. Our strong partnerships with government agencies such as DISCOM, GEDA, and GUNVL, coupled with a robust channel partner network across Gujarat, enable us to extend sustainable energy solutions to diverse sectors.
